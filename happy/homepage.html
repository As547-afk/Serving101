Types of Serve
Standing Float Serve
A serve that has little to no spin, causing it to move unpredictably in the air.
Standing Topspin Serve
A serve that has a forward spin, causing the ball to dip quickly over the net.
Underhand Serve
A serve where the player strikes the ball with an underhand motion, often used by beginners.
Jump Float Serve
A serve where the player jumps and hits the ball with little to no spin, causing it to move unpredictably.
Jump Topspin Serve
A serve where the player jumps and hits the ball with a forward spin for added power and dip.
